The Problem

SYBA needed leads across 3 regions and 6 industries. Manual prospecting wasn’t cutting it.

🌎

3 Regions, 6 Segments, Zero System

SYBA covers Belgium, broader Europe, and the USA. Their ideal customers span Insurance Brokers, Family Offices, Corporate HR, Law Firms, Wealth Managers, and MSPs. Finding qualified leads across all of these manually? Impossible to scale.

⏰

Hours Lost to Prospecting

The team was spending hours every day researching companies, finding decision-makers, writing emails from scratch, and tracking who they’d contacted. Time that should have been spent closing deals.

📄

Generic Lead Data, No Context

Off-the-shelf databases gave names and emails — but no industry context, no trigger events, no reason to reach out. Cold emails felt cold because they were.

📊

No Way to Prioritize

Without fit scoring or intelligence, every lead looked the same. The team couldn’t tell a 9/10 prospect from a 4/10 — so they treated them all equally and wasted time on poor fits.
