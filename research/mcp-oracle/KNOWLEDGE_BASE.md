## Competitive Landscape

### Clay.com
- **What they do:** Waterfall enrichment across 75+ data providers
- **Data Sources:** Clearbit, Apollo, ZoomInfo, Lusha, Hunter, Pipl, FullContact, LinkedIn, and 60+ more
- **Pricing:** $149-800/mo
- **Our Gap:** We use Apollo + Apify + Firecrawl. Missing: Clearbit, ZoomInfo, Lusha, Hunter as direct integrations.
- **Our Advantage:** Custom AI agents, not just enrichment. We generate leads AND outreach, not just data.

### Instantly.ai
- **What they do:** Cold email + lead database
- **Data Sources:** Built-in B2B database, email warming, sequence automation
- **Pricing:** $30-97/mo
- **Our Gap:** We lack email warming infrastructure (but Gmail handles this natively for us).
- **Our Advantage:** We do deep research per prospect, not spray-and-pray.

### Smartlead
- **What they do:** Cold email at scale
- **Data Sources:** Email infrastructure focus, less on data
- **Pricing:** $39-94/mo
- **Our Gap:** None significant -- different approach.
- **Our Advantage:** Quality over quantity.

### Lemlist
- **What they do:** Multi-channel outreach (email, LinkedIn, calls)
- **Data Sources:** Built-in lead database, email finder, LinkedIn automation
- **Pricing:** $39-159/mo
- **Our Gap:** LinkedIn automation (we don't touch it).
- **Our Advantage:** AI-powered personalization, not templates.

### Composio
- **What they do:** 850+ app integrations via MCP protocol
- **Data Sources:** Broad integration layer, not deep on any one source
- **Pricing:** Free tier + paid
- **Our Gap:** They have breadth. We should use their rube MCP for rapid integration testing.
- **Our Advantage:** We have depth. Our agents understand the data, not just pipe it.

### Generect
- **What they do:** AI outbound lead generation
- **Data Sources:** Multiple enrichment sources, AI personalization
- **Pricing:** Custom
- **Our Gap:** They may have better enrichment waterfalls.
- **Our Advantage:** We offer full service (leads + outreach + dashboard), not just lead lists.
