How the Agent Swarm Works

Six specialized AI agents work in parallel to assess every dimension of your security posture.

Analyzes your network perimeter, open ports, DNS configuration, and firewall rules for potential entry points.

Scans for known CVEs, outdated software, misconfigured services, and common security weaknesses.
