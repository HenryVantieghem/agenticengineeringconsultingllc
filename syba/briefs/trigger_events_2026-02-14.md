- **ICP Segments:** Wealth Managers, Family Offices, Insurance Brokers

- **Hook:** "An Australian investment firm just received the first-ever cybersecurity fine under financial services law -- AU$2.5M -- after ALPHV ransomware exposed 18,000 clients' passport and tax data. Regulators are now holding wealth managers personally accountable for cyber failures."
