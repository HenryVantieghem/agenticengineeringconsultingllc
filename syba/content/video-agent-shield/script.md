# Agent Shield — Product Demo Video Script

**Duration:** ~60 seconds
**Style:** Corporate tech / cinematic / premium
**Tone:** Authoritative, forward-looking, urgent

---

## Scene 1 — The Hook (0:00 - 0:08)
**Narration:** "You insure your office. You insure your employees. But who insures your AI agents?"
**Visual:** Slow zoom into a modern glass office building at twilight. Glowing data streams visible through windows. Dramatic lighting.
**Transition:** Cut to black, beat

## Scene 2 — The Problem (0:08 - 0:18)
**Narration:** "Every company is deploying AI agents. But no one is protecting them. No security. No monitoring. No insurance. Until now."
**Visual:** Abstract visualization of AI agents as glowing nodes connecting in a network. One node turns red, flickering — representing a breach. Ripple effect through the network.
**Transition:** Dissolve

## Scene 3 — Agent Shield Introduction (0:18 - 0:30)
**Narration:** "Introducing Agent Shield by SYBA. The world's first AI agent cybersecurity and insurance platform. Eight specialized AI sub-agents analyze your security posture in seconds."
**Visual:** Clean white interface showing the Agent Shield swarm visualization. Eight nodes spinning up around a central core. Each glowing as they activate. Modern, OpenAI-style aesthetic.
**Transition:** Smooth cut

## Scene 4 — How It Works (0:30 - 0:42)
**Narration:** "Prompt injection scanning. Data access auditing. Compliance monitoring. Behavioral anomaly detection. And insurance risk calculation. All in one platform."
**Visual:** Quick montage of dashboard screens — security score ring filling up, category breakdown cards appearing, risk alerts popping up. Clean, minimal UI.
**Transition:** Cut

## Scene 5 — The Insurance (0:42 - 0:50)
**Narration:** "And when something goes wrong? Agent Shield provides up to five million dollars in coverage. Backed by Chubb and Tokio Marine."
**Visual:** Shield icon with "$5M" text, partner logos fading in. Premium, trust-building visual.
**Transition:** Dissolve to white

## Scene 6 — CTA (0:50 - 0:60)
**Narration:** "Agent Shield by SYBA. Secure your AI agents. Insure your future."
**Visual:** SYBA logo + Agent Shield logo. URL: agent-shield-syba.netlify.app. "Try the free demo" CTA button. Clean white background.
**Transition:** Fade to black
