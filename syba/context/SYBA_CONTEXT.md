## What SYBA Does
SYBA is an all-in-one personal cybersecurity platform that PREVENTS cyber events and PROTECTS families when they happen.

### Products
1. **SYBA App** — Scans WiFi network, monitors connected devices, checks emails for data breaches, verifies social media usernames
2. **Router Security** — Identifies vulnerabilities and malware on network connections
3. **24/7 Cyber Security Team** — Round-the-clock human support for data recovery and security questions
4. **Cyber Insurance** — Up to $5 million coverage for ransomware, fraud, identity theft, cyberbullying
5. **Training Plan** — Education on securing home networks, protecting personal and company data
6. **Desktop Application** — Cloud-based cybersecurity with AI-driven analytics

## Email Templates Framework

### Cold Email Structure
1. **Subject:** Tie to trigger event or pain point (never generic)
2. **Opening:** Reference something specific about their company/role
3. **Problem:** One stat that hits their specific pain point
4. **Solution:** How SYBA solves it (1-2 sentences max)
5. **Credibility:** Partnership mention (Chubb/Jencap/PwC)
6. **CTA:** Specific ask (15-min call, demo, resource share)

### Follow-Up Sequence
- **Day 3:** New angle/stat, reference original email
- **Day 7:** Share relevant case study or news article
- **Day 14:** Break-up email, offer resource with no strings
